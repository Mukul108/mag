School History

Founded in 1973, our school was established with the visionary aim of Mr. Bansilal to nurture brilliant stars. Over the years, we have strived to fulfill this vision, providing a platform for students to shine. With each passing year, our school has grown and evolved, empowering young minds to reach their full potential. Today, we stand proud as a beacon of excellence, with a legacy spanning five decades.

The plans to build school ....

we are excited to share our ambitious plans to build a state-of-the-art school facility that will inspire and empower generations to come. Our vision is to create an educational environment that fosters creativity, innovation, and holistic growth. The new school will feature modern classrooms equipped with cutting-edge technology, spacious laboratories for scientific exploration, a well-stocked library to encourage reading and research, and vibrant recreational areas for sports and extracurricular activities. We are committed to incorporating sustainable design principles, ensuring energy efficiency and a harmonious integration with the surrounding environment. With this new school, we aim to provide an exceptional educational experience that prepares our students for a successful future.
